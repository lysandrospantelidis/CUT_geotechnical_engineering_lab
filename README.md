# CUT Geotechnical Engineering Lab

The **CUT Geotechnical Engineering Lab** develops research-driven methods and computational tools for the analysis and design of geotechnical systems, with emphasis on **shallow foundations, deep foundations, retaining structures, and soil–structure interaction**.

The repository hosts a collection of programs, numerical tools, and analytical implementations that support both **research and engineering practice**. These tools aim to provide transparent, physically meaningful, and efficient solutions to classical and advanced geotechnical problems, bridging the gap between theory and application.

The tools included in this repository are primarily intended for:

* Academic research
* Teaching and demonstration purposes
* Engineering evaluation and preliminary design

All developments are based on rigorous theoretical background and are accompanied, where applicable, by published or ongoing research work.
